About Me

Hey! Thanks for visiting my page.

I don't know if you stumbled here on accident, or if you came from my LinkedIn, but please, do be so inclined to stay for as long as you'd like.

I'm Euodia (pronounced you-dear), and I'm a penultimate undergraduate at Singapore Management University, majoring in Information Systems. My track specialization is Digitalization and Cloud Solutions. I am passionate about helping others, whether it be helping companies to digitalize their work processes, or giving a voice to people who need it. If I see an opportunity to lead, I enthusiastically take it. My greatest strength would be my openness to learn and my hunger for self-improvement.

I enjoy working with people and I love writing reports. I enjoy design as well, and love creating a meaningful, simple, yet beautiful interface for users.

In my free time, I am sailing with my club at Raffles Marina, or you can find me at the gym, listening to music or attempting to art.

I'm always open to new opportunities and ideas, so do connect and hit me up!
